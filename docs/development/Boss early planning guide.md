 Now let me create a comprehensive boss design for you. I'll aim for 2-3 bosses per category with a mix of upgraded
  regulars and unique legendaries, using mostly existing skills plus signature moves.

  Quest Board - Boss Monster Design Document

  Design Principles

  - Boss tier (1.06x HP/ATK): Dungeon end bosses, moderate challenge
  - Raid boss tier (1.1x HP/ATK): Legendary encounters, epic battles
  - Skills: 4 skills each - 3 from existing pools + 1 signature ability
  - Visual: Mix of upgraded category creatures and unique legendary beings
