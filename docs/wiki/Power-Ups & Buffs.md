# Power-Ups & Buffs

Power-ups are temporary bonuses earned by completing tasks, maintaining streaks, and hitting milestones. They enhance your productivity by rewarding consistency and celebrating progress.

---

## How Power-Ups Work

Power-ups trigger automatically when you meet specific conditions. There's no manual activationâ€”they just happen when you're doing well!

**The flow:**
1. Complete a task, level up, or build a streak
2. If conditions match a trigger, you earn a power-up
3. The buff appears in your status bar and applies immediately
4. It lasts until its duration expires (hours, uses, or midnight)

## Tiers and Rarity

Power-ups come in three tiers:

| Tier | Power Level | Examples |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **T1** | Common | First Blood, Momentum, Lucky Star |
| **T2** | Uncommon | Flow State, Streak Shield, Level Up! |

## Duration Types

Power-ups expire in different ways:

| Duration | Behavior |
|----------|----------|
| **Hours** | Expires after X hours (e.g., 1 hour, 24 hours) |
| **Uses** | Expires after N task completions (e.g., "next 3 tasks") |
| **Until Midnight** | Expires at 11:59 PM local time |
| **Until Used** | Single-use effect (e.g., Streak Shield blocks one reset) |
| **Passive** | Never expires (class perks only) |

## Collision Policies

What happens when you trigger a power-up you already have?

| Policy | Behavior | Examples |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **Refresh** | Timer resets to full duration | First Blood, Lucky Star |
| **Stack** | Adds stacks; effects multiply | Momentum, Streak Shield |
| **Extend** | Adds more uses/time | Catch-Up |
| **Ignore** | Nothing happens | â€” |

> [!TIP]
> **Momentum stacks!** Each quick quest completion adds another +10% XP layer. Complete 5 one-shot quests and you're at +50% XP until midnight.

## Tips for Maximizing Buffs

### Start Strong
Complete your first task early to get **First Blood** and benefit from +5% XP for your productive morning hours.

### Build Momentum
Knock out quick one-shot quests to stack **Momentum** buffs. Each adds +10% XP for the rest of the day.

### Protect Your Streak
At 3 days, you earn a **Streak Shield**. This forgives one missed dayâ€”but if you're a Paladin, you already get one free!

### Push Through Slumps
The **Phoenix** trigger rewards you for coming back after 3+ days away. Don't feel guilty about breaksâ€”your return is celebrated!
