## Stats System

Each class has two **Primary Stats** that receive +2 bonus at character creation. All stats start at 10.

### The Six Stats

| Stat | Combat Effect | XP Source |
|------|---------------|-----------|
| **Strength** | Physical attack power | Fitness, Exercise, Admin |
| **Intelligence** | Magic attack, Max Mana | Coding, Dev, Study |
| **Wisdom** | Magic defense, Mana pool | Planning, Research, Health |
| **Constitution** | Max HP, Defense | Wellness, Exercise |
| **Dexterity** | Crit chance, Dodge chance | Creative, Efficiency |
| **Charisma** | Retreat success, Social | Social, Networking |

### Stat Growth

Stats grow automatically as you complete quests:
- Every **100 XP** earned in a category adds **+1** to the associated stat
- Stat bonuses are capped at **Level Ã— 2** per stat
- Category-to-stat mapping varies by class

## Choosing Your Class

Consider these questions:
1. **What types of tasks do you do most often?** Match your quests to bonus categories.
2. **Do you value consistency or bursts?** Clerics reward streaks; Rogues reward speed.
3. **What's your playstyle?** Warriors tank; Technomancers deal magic damage.
