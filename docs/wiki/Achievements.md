# Achievements

Achievements are unlockable badges that recognize your progress and milestones. They provide XP bonuses and a visual record of your accomplishments.

---

## How Achievements Work

Achievements unlock automatically when you meet specific conditions. Each one awards bonus XP and displays in the Achievement Hub.

**The flow:**
1. Meet a trigger condition (reach level, complete streak, etc.)
2. Achievement unlocks with a celebratory modal
3. XP bonus awarded immediately
4. Badge appears in your Achievement Hub

---

## Viewing Achievements

### Command Palette

Open the Achievement Hub:
1. Press `Ctrl/Cmd + P`
2. Search for **Quest Board: Achievement Hub**
3. Browse all achievements with progress tracking

### Sidebar Panel

If enabled, an achievements panel appears in the sidebar showing:
- Recently unlocked badges
- Progress bars for in-progress achievements
- Unlock dates

## Trigger Types

Achievements use different trigger conditions:

| Type | How It Works |
|------|--------------|
| **Level** | Unlock when reaching a specific level |
| **Quest Count** | Total quests completed overall |
| **Category Count** | Quests completed in a specific category folder |
| **Streak** | Days of consecutive quest activity |
| **Manual** | Unlock yourself or via special conditions |

| Field | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Progress** | Current value toward target |
| **Target** | Goal to reach |
| **Percentage** | Visual progress bar fill |

## Managing Achievements

### Edit Existing

1. Open Achievement Hub
2. Click an achievement to expand
3. Modify fields and save

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Built-in achievements can be edited but not deleted. Custom achievements can be removed.

### Manual Unlock

For `manual` trigger achievements:
1. Open Achievement Hub
2. Find the achievement
3. Click **Unlock** button

Useful for tracking real-world accomplishments not tied to quests.

### Delete Custom

1. Open Achievement Hub
2. Expand the custom achievement
3. Click **Delete**
